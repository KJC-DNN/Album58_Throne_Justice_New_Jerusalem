# Album 58 - Throne of Justice &amp; New Jerusalem Shines
Proclamation of final judgment and victory of the Lamb.

## Songs:

Song 01 - The Great White Throne - Proclamation of final judgment and victory of the Lamb.

Song 02 - Glory to the Lamb - Celebration of Christ as Light defeating darkness.

Song 03 - Jesus is Coming Back - Simple declaration of Christ's return.

Song 04 - The Donkey's Cry - Humble beast seeking freedom in New Jerusalem.

Song 05 - The Sheep's Song - Shepherd theme and redemption.

Song 06 - The Goat's Dance - Clean animal rejoicing in grace.

Song 07 - The Deer's Thirst - Soul longing for Living Water in the holy city.
